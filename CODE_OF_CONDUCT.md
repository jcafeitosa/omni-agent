# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to making participation in Omni Agent a respectful, harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent and ask for clarification before escalating.
- Focus discussions on technical outcomes.
- Accept feedback and course-correct quickly.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, intimidation, or personal attacks.
- Doxxing, threats, or sustained disruption.
- Publishing private data without explicit consent.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions and communications that violate this Code of Conduct.

## Reporting

Report incidents to: `security@omni-agent.dev` with subject `CoC Report`.
